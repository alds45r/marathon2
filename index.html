Bring Back the Mods

Variable vape mods, often called "variable wattage" or "variable voltage" mods, are popular devices in the vaping community due to their versatility and customization options. Unlike traditional vape pens with fixed settings, variable mods allow users to adjust the power output, either by modifying the wattage or voltage. This customization enables vapers to fine-tune their experience, tailoring vapor production, flavor intensity, and throat hit to their personal preferences. These mods often feature advanced components such as OLED screens, temperature control, and safety protections, like overheat and short-circuit protection. This makes them ideal for both beginner and experienced vapers seeking a balanced mix of safety, control, and enhanced performance.

Features

Adjustable Wattage and Voltage

Allows users to set their desired wattage or voltage, affecting the intensity and warmth of the vapor. Provides a customizable experience for both beginners and advanced users by catering to individual preferences.

Safety Features

Includes built-in protections like short-circuit protection, overheat protection, low-voltage protection, and overcharge protection. Ensures safe vaping and prolongs the lifespan of the device.

Dual or Single Battery Options

Some mods support dual batteries, increasing power output and battery life, while others are compact single-battery options for portability. Rechargeable through USB-C or external chargers, depending on the mod.
